About

Welcome to the website of Tierney Photography. This business was created in 2020 after I got into photography in a really big way. I started off with a simple smartphone, going out into the countryside and taking pictures of anything that took my fancy.

Now, as you can see, I take photos as my full time job. By supporting this website you will be supporting me directly.

My name is Daniel Tierney and I am a professional photographer.

I grew up in the grey skied town of Oldham, working where ever I could to get by. During those periods I would go out into the countryside and take pictures as a hobby. Due to support from friends/family I made the big leap to doing it full time, and it's working out so far.

I love long walks on the beach and anything greyscale.
